Maxim Gurkin

Helping to deliver best search experience

I’m a UX designer with background in software development. The knowledge of how things work under the hood helps me to deliver better user experience and avoid pitfalls which reveals on a design implementation phase.

For the last 4 years I’ve been focused on search technologies and search-driven applications. My vision of search can be perfectly described by Donald Rumsfeld's quote:

“Reports that say that something hasn't happened are always interesting to me, because as we know, there are known knowns; there are things we know we know. We also know there are known unknowns; that is to say we know there are some things we do not know. But there are also unknown unknowns – the ones we don't know we don't know.”

I am working on all these directions helping people to find known knowns (navigation), known unknowns (search by cretiria) and unknown unknowns (discovery).

In a working process I highly value two basic principals: 'keep things simple' and 'there is no way to overcommunicate'. I am always happy to work directly with customers and users to make the process transparent and avoid sudden issues.
